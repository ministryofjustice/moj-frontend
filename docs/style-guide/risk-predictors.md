---
title: risk predictors
---

Use ‘risk predictors’ to describe the tools that calculate how likely it is that a person will reoffend. The old set of risk predictors included RSR and OGRS. A new set of risk predictors, with new names, was released in February 2026.

Use the full name of the risk predictor, where possible, rather than an acronym or initialism. The names for the new risk predictors describe the type of reoffending they predict. Acronyms and initialisms do not help users understand how to use the risk predictors.

Use sentence case for the names of the specific risk predictors:

- All reoffending predictor  
- Violent reoffending predictor  
- Combined serious reoffending predictor  
- Serious violent reoffending predictor 
- Direct contact -- sexual reoffending predictor 
- Images and indirect contact -- sexual reoffending predictor
