title: No results (from search)
---

Show the following heading after a user has conducted a search (with their search term in single quote marks):  

0 results for ‘Tomm Wilson’ in open cases.

Help the user to refine their search. Depending on your product and the search, this could include guidance on:  

- searching under a different category, for example ‘identity number’ rather than ‘name’
- the correct formats for search terms, for example whether spelling needs to be exact
- a route to contact support, or raise a ticket

Work to prevent errors, for example by considering validation carefully. View [GOV.UK guidance on helping users give the right information](https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/design/designing-good-questions#:~:text=service%20too%20complicated.-,Help%20users%20give%20you%20the%20right%20information,and%20write%20good%20error%20messages).
